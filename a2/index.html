About Us

Cinemax is a movie theatre that was established to serve local movie enthuasiasts.

Eventhough Cinemax was founded a long time ago, our facilites has recently been renovated to make the experience of movie going even more enjoyable.

More comfortable seating for a better movie viewing experience

Our goal is for you to have an immersive movie experience, with that in mind, we would like to introduce you to our newly equipped seats with better cushioning system. We acknowledge that movies' durations can be extensive, these newly upgraded seats will ensure your comfortability and prevent lumbar and back pain.



Standard Seating

Our new standard seats will now come with cupholders so you will never have to worry out spilling your drinks ever again. This seat is mad with high quality faux leather to bring you an enjoyable movie exprience.
